KOLLAM

The district has a cross-section of Kerala's natural attributes.

it is endowed with a long coastline, a major Laccadive Sea seaport and an inland lake (Ashtamudi Lake).

The district has many water bodies. Kallada River is one among them.

Ashramam Tourist Village is a well-known tourist destination in Kollam city; the tourist village is located on the banks of

Ashtamudi Lake. Ashramam is a hub of tourism activities in Kollam, and the District Tourism Promotion Council (DTPC) office is

located within the village.
