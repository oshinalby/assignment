KOTTAYAM

The district comprises six municipal towns:

Kottayam, Changanassery, Pala, Erattupetta, Ettumanoor, and Vaikom.

It is the only district in Kerala that neither border the Arabian Sea nor any other states.

Kottayam has a network of rivers, backwaters, ancient religious places, and hill stations.

Local tourist places include:kumarakom,Vembamad Lake,vagamon,illikal kallu
