KASARAGOD

Kasaragod is the northernmost district of Kerala .

Also known as Saptha Bhasha Sangama Bhoomi (The land of seven languages) as seven languages namely, Malayalam, Tulu,

Kannada, Marathi, Konkani, Beary, and Urdu are spoken, unlike the other districts of Kerala.

Tourist places include kottappuram walking bridge, Chandragiri fort, Bekal fort etc.
